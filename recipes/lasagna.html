Lasagna

Description

Traditional dish of Italian cuisine that is prepared with overlapping sheets of pasta, filled with a sauce that usually contains meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder Garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons of MAGGI® Beef Broth
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1 sachet of MAGGI® Bolognese Tomato Sauce
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 3/4 pound cooked lasagna pasta Pre-cooked lasagna pasta
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a bowl, place the meat, garlic powder, MAGGI® 2 Beef Broth, and ground pepper and marinate for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large saucepan, place the oil, onion, garlic, and celery and sauté for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the meat and cook for 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. When the meat acquires a light gray hue, add the MAGGI® Bolognese Style Tomato Sauce, water, bay leaves, salt and pepper and cook over medium heat for 15 minutes.
  5. Assembling the lasagna: When assembling the lasagna, grease a refractory container or the tray you use with a little oil. Place a small amount of sauce, cover the bottom of the tray with pre-cooked pasta slices, pour a small amount of meat, add the mozzarella cheese and a minimal part of Parmesan cheese.
  6. Repeat this procedure until the container is filled. Finally, place a layer of pre-cooked pasta, the remaining sauce and cover with the cheeses. The lasagna has to have a good amount of liquid for the pasta to cook. If it is very dry, you can add chicken broth.
  7. Cover it with aluminum foil and place in the oven at 150°C for 40 minutes.
  8. Additional step: You can add black olives and basil leaves to this recipe when cooking the meat.
